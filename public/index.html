3rd National Hip Course & INDOCON 2026

The 3rd National Hip Course & 23rd INDOCON 2026 advances hip preservation, arthroplasty, biomechanics, and orthopaedic innovation—bringing together leading clinicians, researchers, and industry experts.

Expect cadaveric workshops, simulation labs, robotics, interactive discussions, and lectures by eminent national & international faculty.

  • 50+ faculties (national & international)
  • Hands-on cadaveric & simulation labs
  • Strong focus on mentorship & networking
Welcome Message

We’re delighted to welcome you

It is our great pleasure to welcome you to the 3rd National Hip Course and 23rd INDOCON 2026 to be held from 23rd to 25th January 2026 in the vibrant city of Indore – the heart of Madhya Pradesh.

This year’s conference promises to be an academic feast, bringing together the finest minds in orthopaedics from across India and the globe. With hands-on cadaveric workshops, advanced simulation labs, robotics demonstrations, case-based interactive sessions, and enlightening lectures by renowned national and international faculty, INDOCON 2026 is designed to inspire learning, encourage innovation, and foster collaboration.

Indore, with its rich culture, warm hospitality, and world-famous culinary delights, offers the perfect backdrop for this academic journey. Beyond scientific exchange, the conference provides an invaluable opportunity to network, share ideas, and build partnerships that will shape the future of orthopaedics.

On behalf of the Organizing Committee, we warmly invite you to join us in Indore to be a part of this exciting academic and cultural experience. Let us come together to learn, connect, and advance the boundaries of hip surgery and orthopaedic excellence.

We look forward to welcoming you to 3rd NHC INDOCON 2026.
Warm Regards,

Welcome from the Organising Team

We’re excited to host you in Indore for a memorable, practice-changing experience.

Dear Colleagues and Friends,

It is our pleasure to invite you to INDOCON 2026, the 23rd Annual Conference of the Association of Orthopaedic Surgeons of Indore (AOSI). Our scientific program spans the latest in hip arthroplasty, trauma, deformities, robotics, and simulation-based learning—delivered by renowned national and international faculty.

Indore—celebrated for its warmth, culture, and world-famous cuisine—offers the perfect backdrop for learning, collaboration, and fellowship. We look forward to welcoming you for three inspiring days of ideas, skills, and connections.
